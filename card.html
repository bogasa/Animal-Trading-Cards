Clownfish

A layer of mucus on the clownfish's skin makes it ummune to the fish-eating anemone's lethal sting

Scientific Name: Amphiprioninae
Average Lenght: 11 centimeters
Average Lifespan: 7 years
Habitat: Tropical coral reefs

Bright orange with three distinctive white bars, clown anemonefish are among the most recognizable of all reef-dwellers. They reach about 11 centimeters in lenght, and are named for the multicolored sea anemone in which they make their homes.
